Private Sunrise Taj Mahal Tour from Delhi by Car - All Inclusive
Early Morning Departure
The Sunrise tours from Delhi to Taj Mahal usually begins very early in the morning, between 2:30 AM to 3:00 AM, from your hotel in Delhi or a designated meeting point.
You will travel from Delhi to Agra by a private car with our tourist driver. Journey takes approximately 3.5 hrs. on early hours.
Arrival in Agra
You'll arrive in Agra before sunrise, ensuring that you reach the Taj Mahal in time for the sunrise view.
Taj Mahal Sunrise visit: The highlight of the tour is witnessing the Taj Mahal during sunrise. Your guide will lead you into the Taj Mahal complex, providing insights into its history and architecture as you enjoy the breathtaking views. Breakfast: After the sunrise visit, you'll be treated to a complimentary breakfast at an hotel in Agra.
Agra Fort - Lunch - Shopping(Optional)
Visit the Agra Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage site with historical significance and impressive architecture. Shopping (Optional): Visits to local markets or shops where you can buy souvenirs and Agra’s famous marble handicrafts.
You'll have lunch at a local restaurant or hotel in Agra, where you can savor traditional Indian cuisine
You have option to buy souvenirs and Agra’s famous marble handicrafts.
Return to Delhi
After completing the Sunrise tour of Taj Mahal, you will head back to Delhi in your private car
Drop Off: Your trip to Taj mahal for sunrise from Delhi conclude with a drop-off at your hotel or a designated location in Delhi
Note: Trip to Taj Mahal Agra do not operates on Fridays
