Taj Mahal Full Moon Night View Tour from Delhi
Day:1 Departure from Delhi
Our driver to meet you at hotel or a pre-arranged meeting point in Delhi and proceed to Agra.
Driving from Delhi to Agra takes 3.5 to 4 hours by car which covers a distance of 225 kilometers through Yamuna Express way, one of the best highways in India and offers a smooth ride. Our driver will stop the car in midway to use facilities
Evening Arrival in Agra
Upon reaching Agra check-in at pre-booked hotel and have some free time to relax and freshen up.
Dinner - Pre Tour Briefing - Security Check - Entry to Taj Mahal - Guided Tour - Moonlight - Quite Contemplation
Day 2: Agra Fort - Fatehpur Sikri visit
After breakfast at the hotel, you will visit Agra Fort features impressive architecture and beautiful pavilions. Listen as your guide explains details on this red fort's beautiful palaces as well as its origins and history.After completion of your visit Agra Fort drive to Fatehpur Sikri which is 43 kms. from Agra approximate 1.15 hrs. drive.
A UNESCO World Heritage site and a historical city built by Emperor Akbar. Fatehpur Sikri (the City of Victory) was the capital of the Mughal Empire for only some 10 years.
Return to Delhi
Lunch
