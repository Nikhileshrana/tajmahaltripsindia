Delhi to Taj Mahal & Fort Private Day Trip by Express Train with Lunch
Delhi – Agra Express Train
Your one-day trip to the Taj Mahal from Delhi begins with an early morning pick up from your hotel or a designated meeting point in Delhi at 7 AM and transfer to the Hazrat Nizamuddin Railway station to board the train for Agra.
Gatiman Express train one of the fastest trains between Delhi and Agra departs at 08.10 AM from Hazrat Nizamuddin Railway station takes 2 hours to reach Agra, you will enjoy comfortable seating and light breakfast, bottled water on the train.
Arrival in Agra
Upon reaching Agra, our Driver/Guide meets you at the railway station waiting for you with your name on a pla card mentioning your name on it, accompany you throughout the day.
Taj Mahal visit: Your first stop on day trip to taj mahal from Delhi by private car will be the Taj Mahal, our experience professional guide will provide insights into its history, architecture and significance as you explore this stunning monument.
Lunch 1 PM : After the Taj Mahal visit enjoy a traditional Indian lunch at a 5* hotel.
Agra Fort visit
Visit the Agra Fort, another UNESCO World Heritage site with historical significance and impressive architecture. Shopping (Optional): Visits to local markets or shops where you can buy souvenirs and Agra’s famous marble handicrafts.
In the late afternoon, you will be transferred back to the Agra Cantt Railway station
Evening Return
You will board the express train back to Delhi, departs from Agra Cantt railway station at 17:45 hours and reach Delhi with an estimated arrival time in Delhi at 19:30hrs.
You will be met with our drive on arrival at the Railway station and transfer to the hotel or designated place. Your one-day trip to Taj Mahal from Delhi by express train conclude here.
Note: Trip to Taj Mahal Agra do not operates on Fridays

  • Stay hydrated and protect yourself from the sun especially during the summer months
