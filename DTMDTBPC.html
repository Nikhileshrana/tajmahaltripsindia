Delhi Taj Mahal Day Tour By Private Car
Departure From Delhi
Our driver to meet you at hotel or a pre-arranged meeting point in Delhi and your tour typically starts early in the morning 6 to 7 AM to beat the traffic and make the most of your day.
Driving from Delhi to Agra takes 3.5 to 4 hours by car which covers a distance of 225 kilometers through Yamuna Express way one of the best highways in India and offers a smooth ride. Our driver will stop the car in midway to use facilities, you can opt for light breakfast here.
Arrival in Agra
As you reach Agra, use facilities or can have breakfast at hotel / Restaurant. Proceed to visit the Taj Mahal
Taj Mahal Agra Visit
Enjoy your visit to Taj Mahal, spend a few hours exploring the most iconic wonders in the world, our professional Guide provide you with historical and cultural insights about the Taj Mahal. You will have the opportunity to learn about Taj History, architecture, and the love story behind its creation.
Take your time, admire the intricate details and take plenty of photos.
Lunch Break - Agra Fort - Return to Delhi
After visiting Taj Mahal, enjoy hot lunch at a 5* hotel in Agra
After Lunch, explore Agra Fort another UNESCO World Heritage site nearby. Take your time, admire the intricate details and take plenty of photos.
Depending on your schedule, you may stop at some shops or markets for souvenir shopping on the way back. After exploring Agra, you will head back to Delhi in your private car
Tips
  • Wear comfortable clothing and footwear suitable for walking
  • Stay hydrated and protect yourself from the sun especially during the summer months
  • Carry some Indian currency for entrance (if not included in your tour package), food and souvenirs
